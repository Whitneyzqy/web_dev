U-Safe

Your safety is our priority.

College campuses provide students with various recreational events, such as sports games, field trips, and club events. Also, off-campus parties and concerts held by the local community and businesses offer great opportunities for students to have fun and release academic pressure from course assignments. However, when students participate in these recreational activities, there is an uncertainty in personal security.

Though in recent years colleges have set up certain safety alerts to notify students of crime or violent incidents via cell phone messages and emails, these warning messages are invalid or outdated when dealing with the changing situation. When students receive the alert information about crime incidents nearby, they could not track and visualize the real-time location of these incidents, which students may have difficulties in forming a clear idea about how close they are from these incidents.
